Blood and Glory

A drama about friendship between two homeless veterans on the streets of LA.

Director's Note

Period poverty wasn't something I ever thought about until one day, while volunteering on Skid Row, I saw a veteran walking down the street with a large period stain on her back. This story is really close to my heart because I'm a combat veteran myself.
